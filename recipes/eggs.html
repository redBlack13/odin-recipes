Creamy Scrambled Eggs

This recipe will demonstrate how to cook the creamiest scrambled eggs you have ever tasted. There are no big secrets in this recipe, just good cooking techniques executed well.

Ingredient

  • 3 Large eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 2 pieces of sour dough bread

Steps

  1. Place your table spoon of butter into your saucepan
  2. Place saucepan over stove on medium low heat and wait for butter to start to melt
