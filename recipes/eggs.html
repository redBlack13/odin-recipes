Creamy Scrambled Eggs

This recipe will demonstrate how to cook the creamiest scrambled eggs you have ever tasted. There are no big secrets in this recipe, just good cooking techniques executed well.

Ingredient

  • 3 Large eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 2 pieces of sour dough bread

Steps

  1. Place your table spoon of butter into your saucepan
  2. Cook your Sourdough toast at this point, your hands will be full in a minute
  3. Place saucepan over stove on medium low heat and wait for butter to start to melt
  4. Once the butter has started to melt, crack your 3 eggs directly into the saucepan
  5. Take a wooden spoon or spatula and begin stirring the eggs into the butter
  6. This step is crucial, DO NOT Stop stirring your eggs. The aim is to mix continuously to prevent large curds from forming. THis will ensure your eggs have a creamy texture
  7. If required move your saucepan on and off the heat to regulate cooking time and ensure the eggs are evenly cooked
  8. Eventually your eggs will start to hold shape and form small lumps, with an almost mousse like texture. When you think they may be slightly underdone they are perfect!
  9. Serve with Sour Dough Toast and salt and pepper
