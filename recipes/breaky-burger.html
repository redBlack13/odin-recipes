Breaky Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of bacon
  • 1 cup of onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of mayo
  • 1 Sourdough bun

Steps

    Preheat large frying pan over medium high heat and add vegetable oil Cut tomato in half and season on both sides with salt and pepper
  1. Add tomato and bacon to pan, cooking evenly on both sides.
  2. Cook tomato until slightly charred on each side. Cook bacon to preferred level of crispness
  3. Remove tomato and bacon and place on plate to cool
  4. Crack egg into pan and cook until preferred yolk level of doneness
  5. Flip egg and place slice of cheese on top, cooking until cheese begins to melt
  6. Spread mayo onto roll before placing cooked ingredients onto roll
