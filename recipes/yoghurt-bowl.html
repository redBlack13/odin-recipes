Yoghurt Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of yogurt
  • 1 cup of blueberries
  • 1 cup of muesli
  • 1 cup of blackberries
  • 1 tablespoon of honey

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, add the yogurt
  2. Add the blueberries into a large bowl
  3. Pour the blackberries into the large bowl
  4. Pour the muesli into a large bowl
  5. drizzle honey over contents of the bowl
